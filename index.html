Welcome!

I am a PhD student at the University of Udine under the supervision of professor Alberto Marcone. I graduated in mathematics at the University of Trento in 2021 with the thesis Model theory of pseudofinite structures. Here is my CV.

I am interested in computability theory, with a focus on foundational questions from mathematical logic. My research so far focused on the reverse mathematics of combinatorial principles, especially from order theory and graph theory. I am also interested in Ramsey theory, in particular in the study of Ramsey like statements.

For more information about my research, please refer to my research statement.
